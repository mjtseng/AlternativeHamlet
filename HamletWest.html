Sailing West?

You ask a sailor about hitching a ride west. He looks at you like you're stupid and asks why you want to sail west.
Before you can answer, he recognizes you as Hamlet, Prince of Denmark, and takes you back to the palace for the trial.

You realize that you can't sail west. That would be sailing further inland into Denmark. The sailor was right, you are stupid.
